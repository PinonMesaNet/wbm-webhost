We welcome you to Widefield Baptist Mission, located in Jones Ranch, New Mexico which is part of the Chichiltah Community. Established in the 1950's and part of the Baptist Mid-Mission, we are committed to biblical preaching, fervent prayer, and loving our neighbors.  Whether you are from here, or from anywhere else, you are family here with us.
