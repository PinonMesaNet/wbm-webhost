Ministries

Whispering Cedars
Summer Camp &
Church Camp

Our camp ministry started back in the 1960’s  where  we have 5 days set apart minister to the young people from the grades of 3 to 12th.  We teach them how to know and serve the God of the Bible, enjoy building relationships through fun activities, and sing loud praises to our great God while shower them with Christ’s love.  Some campers receive the Lord as their Savior or commit themselves to a more faithful walk with Him. We still hear testimonies of older people who got saved at church and the camp ministry many years ago.

Bible Study

We have a Ladies Bible Study, Ladies’ prayer,
one on one visiting and conversations,
Bible study and Discipleship training,
plus counseling for people in need. 

Work Projects

During the spring and fall our ministry includes time devoted to maintaining the property and preparing it for our camp. There is always a need for groups, individuals, and retiree teams to help with projects like painting, remodeling, roofing, yard maintenance, cleaning, winterizing, and small engine repair. A new project that we are planning is to remodel the original chapel building, which has been used for storage in the last decade, into a rustic rec center for our youth to play in. 

Community Outreach

We work with Individuals on projects at the Church and at their homes.
Our goal is to evangelizing the unsaved, and teach and train those who are saved to faithfully serve Christ in the Church with the Spiritual gifts with which they have been gifted.
